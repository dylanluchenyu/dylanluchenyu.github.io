HKO Flight Weather Dashboard is a small dashboard for HKIA flight situational awareness. It combines public arrival and departure data with aviation weather reports such as METAR and TAF.

The project supports time-horizon views, arrival and departure summaries, and aviation weather status checks for route airports.
